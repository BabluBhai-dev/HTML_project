1. Introduction

The X-Pro Smart Gadget is designed to improve your daily productivity. It is lightweight, durable, and extremely simple to use.

2. Setup Guide

  1. Unpack the device from the box.
  2. Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Connect to Wi-Fi for the best experience.

3. Operating Instructions

Button Functions

Power Button
Turn device ON/OFF
Menu Button
Access main features
Volume Keys
Increase / Decrease volume

Daily Use

Ensure the device is charged. Navigate using the on-screen menu to access features.

4. Safety Guidelines

  • Keep device away from water and fire.
  • Use only original accessories.
  • Keep away from reach of children.
  • Turn off when boarding flights.

5. Troubleshooting

Issue Possible Solution
Device not turning on Charge for at least 10 minutes and try again.
No sound Increase volume or restart the device.
Frozen screen Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds.
