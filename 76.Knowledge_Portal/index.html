Enterprise Service Mesh Blueprint

Summary: Design pattern for zero-trust, policy-driven service-to-service communication across hybrid clusters.

Authors: Anita Desai , Rahul Mehta

Identifiers: ARCH-DR-0127 | Published: 12 Sep 2025 | Reviewed: 05 Nov 2025

Adopt sidecar proxies, centralized policy, mTLS, and declarative routing. Use layered SLOs and golden paths to balance resilience and cost. Include fail-open strategies only for non-critical telemetry.

Tags: service-mesh, security, mTLS, SLOs

Data Platform Governance Model

Summary: Federated governance with product-aligned domains, contract-first schemas, and lineage-aware SLAs.

Authors: Sanjana Iyer

Identifiers: DATA-GOV-0034 | Published: 20 Jul 2025 | Reviewed: 28 Oct 2025

Define domain councils, enforce schema contracts at ingress, and track lineage at transformation points. SLA tiers must include freshness, accuracy, and completeness. Non-conformant feeds are quarantined until remediated.

Tags: data-mesh, governance, SLA, lineage

Enable mTLS Across Clusters

Step-by-step process to configure mutual TLS between services in hybrid environments.

Estimated time: INR 0 | 45 minutes

Cluster credentials, CA root bundle, policy manifests

  1. Validate trust anchors
    Confirm CA fingerprints and expiry dates match security policy.
  2. Apply sidecar policy
    Attach mutual TLS policy to workloads via deployment manifests.
  3. Test handshake
    Use health endpoints to verify certificates are presented and validated.

Outcome: Encrypted, verified service communication
