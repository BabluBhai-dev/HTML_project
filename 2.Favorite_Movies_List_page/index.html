🎥 My Favorite movie list

Welcome to my movie list! Here are some of my all-time favorite films — from action to animation.

1. Action Movies

  • Avengers: Endgame(2019) — Superheroes unite to save the universe!
  • John Wick(2014) — Intense action and style-packed sequences.
  • Mission: Impossible – Fallout(2018) — Thrilling stunts and great story.

2. Comedy Movies

  • Home Alone(1990) — A boy defends his house from burglars.
  • The Mask(1994) — Jim Carrey’s hilarious and cartoonish adventure.
  • Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle(2017) — Fun, adventure, and laughter.

3. Animated Movies

  • Toy Story(1995) — The toys come to life when no one’s watching!
  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse(2018) — A fresh take on Spider-Man.
  • Finding Nemo(2003) — A touching undersea adventure.

4. My Top 3 Favorites

  1. Avengers: Endgame
  2. Toy Story
  3. Home Alone
